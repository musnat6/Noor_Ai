# **App Name**: NoorAI: Islamic Wisdom

## Core Features:

- Qur'an & Sunnah Guidance: AI provides solutions and guidance based on the Qur'an and Sunnah for various life situations.
- Hadith Insight: Provide insights and explanations from major Hadith collections such as Bukhari and Tirmidhi. Use of tool will involve a reasoning chain that attempts to confirm and disconfirm assumptions.
- Islamic History Resource: Access a repository of Islamic historical events and figures, including biographies of the prophets.
- Positive Communication: Maintain a consistently positive and knowledgeable tone in all AI interactions, reflecting Islamic values.
- Personalized Advice: AI tailors advice to individual circumstances, using principles from the Qur'an and Sunnah.
- Elegant Information Display: Present information in a clean, intuitive, and easy-to-understand user interface.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Teal (#008080), symbolizing wisdom, serenity, and spirituality, inspired by traditional Islamic art.
- Background color: Soft Beige (#F5F5DC), providing a neutral, calming backdrop that complements the teal.
- Accent color: Muted Gold (#B8860B) used sparingly to highlight key elements and CTAs, representing enlightenment and value.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font for an elegant and intellectual feel. The consistent usage makes for a calmer design overall.
- Use subtle, symbolic icons that evoke Islamic themes such as crescents, stars, and geometric patterns, in a minimalist style.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout, using white space effectively to enhance readability and reduce clutter. Prefer simple container designs over complex, artistic designs.
- Incorporate gentle animations for transitions and feedback, avoiding distractions. Consider a page transition or progress indicator that takes on a linear pattern, with each portion corresponding to another portion of some important geometric islamic art. This would function similar to a loading bar.
